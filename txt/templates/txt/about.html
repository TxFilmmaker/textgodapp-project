Welcome to the

My name is Will Nicholson, and I’m a husband and recent father of 3 beautiful children. By “recent”, I mean to say that my wife and I are in the process of a multi-child adoption which was the result of the tragic and untimely death of the childrens’ single father. This event, which finds me in my 40s, has brought upon me a whole new perspective of life, responsibility, and a great respect for the minuscule amount of time we have on this Earth before going home to judgement. It’s made me realize that if I were to pass on today, I would likely not be very proud of what I’ve left behind for the world.

I created this site as a means of learning a little more about the bible, while also developing new skills in web design and programming. I'm not a web developer by trade, and in fact have only dabbled in such efforts over the last few decades. However, it’s becoming increasingly obvious that my skills as a professional Visual Effects Artist in the movie/TV industry are quickly becoming obsolete in the face of expanding competition across the globe, a massive influx of new talent from colleges and trade schools, and incredible new emerging technologies. The web design industry is facing a similar fate, however, it’s the programming side of the business that intrigues me, and this website was created as a means of advancing my training in those pursuits.

Nevertheless, I hope you find something interesting, inspiring, or perhaps helpful on this site, as I believe it can truly help bring about a greater understanding of the bible through direct searches for meaningful and familiar words or phrases.

I’ll try to periodically update the functionality here, and make improvements along the way.

In the meantime, thanks for visiting and please enjoy your stay here.

-Will Nicholson
